\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Manages an ordered collection of filters for sequential processing. 

\doxylink{classcgl_1_1FilterPipeline}{Filter\+Pipeline} provides a way to chain multiple filters together and execute them in sequence. It supports adding, removing, and iterating through filters, as well as runtime enable/disable of individual filters.
